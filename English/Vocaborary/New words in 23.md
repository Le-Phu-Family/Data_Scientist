	Eg: The train is full of the commuters every morning/One part of the downtown that the commuters are still moving is the intersection of ThuDuc.

	Eg: I've been meaning to ask you for some advice about my program

	Eg: I'm supposed to finish my deadlines by 5 pm/ Students are supposed to wear uniforms.
